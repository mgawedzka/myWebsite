MichelAngelo

Italian Artist (1475 - 1564)

Renaissance sculptor, painter, architect, and poet

Famous Works in the Sistine Chapel, St Peters Basilica,Vatican City

Michelangelo di Lodovico Buonarroti Simoni was hired by the Pope Julius II to illuminate the Sistine Chapel.

His breathtaking frescoes show the episodes from the Old Testmament: The Creation, the Garden of Eden, and the Flood.

He uses bold colors and striking composition expressing power and emotion.

The Sistine ceiling and the “Last Judgment” stand as a testament to Michelangelo’s genius as a painter and evolution as an artist.
